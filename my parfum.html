Our Story

Born on the Swahili coast, king Parfum marries traditional attars with contemporary perfumery. We source responsibly, distill patiently, and blend for character, not just projection.

2017 — First attar experiments with local oud and clove.
2020 — Launch of Noir; first 300 bottles sell out in a week.
2024 — Stone Town atelier opens; refill program begins.
2026 — Global shipping & limited artist collaborations.
